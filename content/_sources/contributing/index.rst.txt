Contributing

Instructions to develop/get started with `Burr`! If you don't know where
to start, you can always reach out to us:
- Start an issue or discussion on our `GitHub  <https://github.com/apache/burr>`_.
- Reach out to us by emailing `email  <mailto:dev@burr.apache.org>`_ -- you'll want to join the mailing list.
